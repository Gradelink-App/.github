## About Gradelink
Gradelink is a comprehensive school management platform designed to streamline administrative tasks, academic tracking, and communication for K-12 schools, private institutions, and educational organizations. The platform integrates all aspects of school operations into a unified system, including student information management, gradebook functionality, attendance tracking, and parent communication tools. Available for both MacOS and Windows, Gradelink modernizes traditional school administration by replacing paper-based processes with digital solutions that improve efficiency, enhance communication, and provide real-time insights into student performance and school operations.

The platform's academic management features include customizable grading scales, assignment tracking, report card generation, and transcript management that adapt to different educational approaches and curriculum requirements. Gradelink provides attendance tracking with automated notifications, behavior incident reporting, and health record management to ensure comprehensive student oversight. With parent and student portals, mobile accessibility, and communication tools that keep families informed about academic progress and school activities, the platform creates a connected educational ecosystem that supports student success while reducing administrative burden for teachers and school staff.
